Creamy Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi with Linguine

Description

Creamy Lemon Garlic Shrimp Scampi with Linguine is a classic Italian dish that is bursting with flavor. Tender shrimp are sautéed with garlic and butter, then simmered in a luscious sauce of white wine, lemon juice, and zest. Finished with fresh parsley and Parmesan cheese, this dish is tossed with linguine pasta for a comforting and elegant meal. The bright, garlicky flavors and creamy texture make this a perfect weeknight dinner or a special occasion dish.

Ingredients

  • shrimp
  • linguine pasta
  • butter
  • garlic
  • white wine
  • lemon juice and zest
  • parsley
  • parmesan cheese
  • salt
  • pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook linguine according to package directions.
  2. Sauté garlic in butter until fragrant.
  3. Add shrimp and cook until pink and opaque.
  4. Deglaze pan with white wine and lemon juice.
  5. Stir in lemon zest, parsley, and Parmesan.
  6. Toss with cooked linguine and serve.
