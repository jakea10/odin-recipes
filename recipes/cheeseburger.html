Classic American Cheeseburger

A true American icon, the cheeseburger is a simple yet satisfying meal that's perfect for any occasion. Juicy beef patties are grilled to perfection and topped with melted cheese, crisp lettuce, tomato, onion, and tangy pickles, all nestled between soft, toasted buns. Whether you're firing up the grill or cooking on the stovetop, this classic recipe is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients:

  • Ground beef (80/20 chuck is ideal)
  • Hamburger buns
  • Sliced cheese (American, cheddar, Swiss, your choice)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion (red or white)
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise (optional)
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Form patties: Divide ground beef into equal portions and shape into patties slightly larger than buns. Make a small indentation in the center of each patty.
  2. Season: Season both sides of patties with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook: Cook patties on preheated grill or skillet over medium-high heat for 3-5 minutes per side, or until desired doneness. Add cheese slices during the last minute of cooking.
  4. Toast buns: Lightly toast the hamburger buns.
  5. Assemble: Spread ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise (if using) on bottom buns. Top with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, the cooked patty with melted cheese, and the top bun.
